Audio design

The audio design of The School is designed to enhance the player's experience and immerse them in the game's world. The game features a range of sound effects and music that reflect the game's theme and mood. The audio design is carefully crafted to be non-intrusive, allowing players to focus on the core gameplay mechanics.

Level design

The School features a series of progressively challenging levels, each representing a different aspect of introducing game-based learning to the school system. The levels are designed to be engaging and educational, with a focus on critical thinking and problem-solving. Each level presents a unique challenge, such as convincing skeptical colleagues or addressing technical limitations, and provides players with the opportunity to earn points and virtual currency to unlock new game-based learning resources.

Graphic design

The graphic design of The School is simple and intuitive, allowing players to focus on the core gameplay mechanics. The game features a bright and colorful interface with engaging animations and graphics that reflect the theme of game-based learning. The design of the game is consistent and cohesive, allowing players to easily navigate through the various levels and challenges.

Animation design

Custom game design

The design of The School was carefully crafted to immerse players in the process of introducing game-based learning to the school system. The game's core mechanics revolve around communication, negotiation, and problem-solving, allowing players to experience the real-world challenges of introducing new teaching methods. The game is designed to be accessible to a wide range of players, including educators, parents, and students interested in game-based learning.
